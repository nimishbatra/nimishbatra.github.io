About Me

Hello, my name is Nimish Batra. I'm an Industrial Designer based in Mumbai, India. I'll be graduating from IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay in June 2018 with Master's in Industrial Design. I have a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with over two years of work experience in FMCG and automotive industries.

I passionately create beautiful, immersive and meaningful user experiences through my projects. My interest areas are healthcare, lifestyle, internet of things, consumer electronics and music production. I love to work in a collaborative environment and I'm a deep believer of acheiving perfection through perseverance and dedication.

I would love to hear your feedback about my work!
