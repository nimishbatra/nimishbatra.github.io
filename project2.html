Dynabow Jaw Articulator

Healthcare Design, Dental

Dynabow is a collaborative project to create  a  faithful  reproduction  of  patient's  spatial  relationships  of  the  jaw  via  the  creation  of  a  device  which  helps  to  relate  the  patient’s  dental  arches  to  each  other  and  the  cranium  in  a  static  and  dynamic  state. is a perfect bridge between the Doctor, Patient and the Dental Laboratory. It will also create a synergy between the Doctor and the Laboratory, in the best interest of the patient.











Project Video and Demo















The Concept: Dynabow is a complete Jaw Articulator System, made together with dentists, engineers and patients







Captures all Jaw motions upto accuracy of 0.5 degrees and maps the curve in a 3d co-ordinate system















Prototype with Sensors







The algorithm was made using visual programming in Grasshopper and Arduino controller board which picks data from potentiometers and hall effect sensors and maps on the 3D co-ordinate system
