Halo

Smart Device, Speaker Design, Gesture Interaction

Halo is a powerful bluetooth speaker having a minimalistic design. It has a unique way in which the user interacts to control the speaker. Halo has a capacitive Touch Gesture Control Slider which recognizes finger gestures for all the speaker controls. No need of volume knobs and physical buttons.

Just touch to control.

Halo comes with a bright and high contrast white LCD display to view your track information. It shows Artist and Track Position too. Many colors to choose from. What color is your favorite?
