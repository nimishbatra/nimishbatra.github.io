Glee

OLED Lighting, Lifestyle















What makes us human? We love being taken care of, and sometimes it can be hard to find a way to return the same love and care to another living being. Growing a plant can be an enthralling experience, but in today’s decreasing size of housing and congested living spaces. we often miss out on this opportunity. In many cases, it is not possible to grow plants as sufficient sunlight doesn’t reach the indoors. More importantly, finding a separate space to grow plants can be difficult.

What if sunlight could be mimicked inside the house? I thought of using LG’s OLED light to integrate lighting and gardening for indoor spaces. OLEDs are very accurate in color reciprocation and perfect to have a uniform source of light. This provides a close to natural habitat for the plant to bloom. Glee brings in positive energy in the form of life, the plant and brightens up those dull corners of your home.
